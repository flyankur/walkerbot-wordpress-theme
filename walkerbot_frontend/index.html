Walkerbot Tank Treads started with our own robotics project.

As we attempted to bring our personal project to life, our Team found it so difficult to find affordable, custom robotic tank treads to suit our robotic needs. And we quickly realized we werent alone -- all the po- tential solutions were either too small/too large and expensive.

Thus, out of personal nessecity, our Team pioneered our own Tank Tread design and system. We developed a completely unique linear to continuous molding system (Patent pending) while avoiding expen- sive injection molds -- and yielding custom tank treads with superior strength and flexibility.

And now we aim to share our breakthrough to you.
